Introduction Data Visualization Data Science Introduction Are you struggling to learn R so you can create banging data visualizations and produce data science projects that add value? You are not alone. Analyzing data is now common to a lot of professions that may not have any background in programming or computer science. Are you full of excitement for your new R project but finding it difficult to quickly achieve the results you want and need? Coming from a background in other programming languages may be problematic because R does some things a little bit differently. Are you taking a course in R and finding that you can’t keep up with the class and need some background? Or are you just interested in learning more about R and what R can do? I would like to help you do more with R. I want to inspire you to create your own data visualizations, master data science, or develop your own data stories. Read more
